Book Bat is a free web app for cataloguing your own book collection. Point your phone at a book's barcode and it pulls in the cover, title, author and publisher — building your shelf in seconds. Add ratings, notes, tags and reading status, then search, filter and sort however you like.

And it's truly yours: no account, no cloud, no tracking. Everything stays on your device and works offline, and you can export your shelf to a spreadsheet, Goodreads or LibraryThing whenever you want. Scan it, shelve it, own your data.

Try the [live app](https://bat.junglestar.org?source=rokma.com).

### Under the hood

Two open-source apps: &lt;Book Bat&gt;, a Svelte 5 editor for scanning and managing the library, and &lt;Baobab&gt;, an Astro 6 site for browsing and sharing it. TypeScript, Vite, html5-qrcode barcode scanning, installable PWA, deployed on Cloudflare Workers. [Source on GitHub](https://github.com/junglesta/bookbat).
